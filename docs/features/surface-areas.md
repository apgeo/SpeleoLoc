# Surface areas

[← Back to index](../README.md)

A **surface area** is a named geographic region on the surface — a
karst plateau, a massif, a region — used to group caves.

Surface areas are **optional**. You can run the app without any and
assign none of your caves; they are a convenience for organization
and reporting.

## Fields

- **Title** (required, unique in the database).
- **Description** — free-form text.

## Managing surface areas

**Home → ⋮ → Manage surface areas**.

- **Add surface area** — title + description, save.
- **Edit** — long-press / tap the row.
- **Delete** — removes the area; caves previously assigned become
  unassigned (not deleted).

## Assigning a cave to a surface area

- When creating a cave (**Home → Add cave**).
- When editing a cave (long-press the cave row → Edit).

The surface area's title appears under the cave title in the home
list.

## Surface area vs cave area

- **Surface area** = on the surface, groups **caves**.
- **Cave area** = underground, groups **cave places** inside one cave.

Both can be used together. See
[Caves and cave areas](caves-and-areas.md).
